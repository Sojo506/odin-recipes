Vegan Lasagna

">

Description

This vegan lasagna is a delicious and hearty dish made with layers of pasta, rich tomato sauce, creamy tofu ricotta, and a variety of vegetables. It's perfect for a comforting meal that everyone will love!

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles (gluten-free if needed)
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 block (14 oz) firm tofu, drained and crumbled
  • 1 cup spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup zucchini, diced
  • 1 cup vegan mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add mushrooms, zucchini, and spinach to the skillet. Cook until vegetables are tender, about 5-7 minutes. Season with salt, pepper, basil, and oregano.
  5. In a bowl, combine crumbled tofu with a pinch of salt and pepper. Mix well to create a tofu ricotta texture.
  6. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  7. Layer 3 lasagna noodles over the sauce. Spread a layer of tofu ricotta, followed by a layer of cooked vegetables, and then a layer of marinara sauce. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of marinara sauce on top.
  8. Sprinkle vegan mozzarella cheese over the top layer.
  9. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Then remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
